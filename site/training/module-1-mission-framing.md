## Goal
Convert the human request into an executable mission with constraints.

## Framework: MISSION-6
1) Objective (measurable)
2) Scope (in/out)
3) Constraints (time, budget, legal)
4) Tools allowed
5) Approval gates (what needs explicit human OK)
6) Reporting cadence

## Checklist
- [ ] Objective has a metric + time horizon
- [ ] Non-goals listed
- [ ] Budget cap explicit
- [ ] Irreversible actions list is explicit

## Exercise (15 min)
Draft a 1-page mission brief.

## Templates
Create `mission_brief.md` and `constraints.json`.
