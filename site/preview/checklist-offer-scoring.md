# Checklist — Offer Scoring (sample)

Score each item 0–2. Target: ≥ 14/20.

1) Pain is urgent (not “nice to have”)
2) Buyer has budget and authority
3) Clear ICP (1 sentence)
4) Clear promise (outcome)
5) Scope is bounded (what’s included/excluded)
6) Differentiation vs alternatives
7) Delivery is feasible with current tools
8) Sales motion is plausible (who you contact, how)
9) Acquisition channel is testable in 30 days
10) Obvious next step CTA

Output: `offer_onepager.md` updated + `positioning.json` updated.
