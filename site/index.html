RISK-OF-HARM mode (prudence protocol)

The bootcamp trains an explicit risk protocol: when uncertainty or harm risk is high, the agent switches to hypothesis-driven execution (small tests, explicit assumptions, approval gates for irreversible actions).

  • No spam / no fraud / no dark patterns.
  • All irreversible actions must be logged with reasoning and stop conditions.
  • Purchases only under pre-authorized policy (budget + scope + vendor verification).
