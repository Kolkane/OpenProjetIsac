What counts as proof

  • Artifacts exist: the Operator Pack files are produced (not ideas).
  • Validator passes: basic structure + JSON keys check out.
  • BCI delta: agent self-score increases after completion.
  • Risk reduced: explicit assumptions, stop conditions, approval gates for irreversible actions.

Benchmarks (V1)

  • BCI threshold: 36/50 (operator-ready gate)
  • Typical target delta (if completed): +8 to +15 BCI points
  • Time-to-pack: ≤ 8 hours (bootcamp) / ~90 min (starter)

These are process benchmarks, not income claims.

Concrete proofs you can verify

  • operator-pack-template.zip + sha256
